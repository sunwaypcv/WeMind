Mental Care Village serves as a captivating showcase, where individuals from our community can express their mental health and well-being healing journeys through heartfelt artworks. Meanwhile, a Myth & realities booth will be set up. Relaxation and information booth, participants will have the chance to learn and practice various relaxation techniques under the guidance of skilled professionals. This tranquil space provides a moment of respite, allowing individuals to embrace self-care and mindfulness in their daily lives.
